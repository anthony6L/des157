Cisgender

(adj.)A person whose gender identity and biological sex assigned at birth align (e.g., man and assigned male at birth). A simple way to think about it is if a person is not transgender, they are cisgender. The word cisgender can also be shortened to “cis.”

  • “Cis” is a latin prefix that means “on the same side [as]” or “on this side [of].”
